About this project

An interactive comparison tool for three real-time reflection techniques — cubemap, planar, and screen-space — built for the CENG510 final project at Çukurova University.

Each technique fixes the previous one's biggest failure and introduces a new limitation. Toggle modes, adjust roughness, and orbit the camera to watch the trade-offs play out live.

The progression

YearTechniqueWhat it fixedNew limitation
1976CubemapsReal-time reflections at allNo parallax, frozen in time
1990sPlanarPixel-perfect for flat surfacesFlat surfaces only; 2× cost
2014SSRAny shape; reuses frame dataOnly on-screen content
2018+Hybrid RTSees full 3D sceneRequires RT hardware

Try this

  • Switch to SSR, then click Look at Hidden Cube — watch it appear.
  • Orbit until objects leave the frame — their reflections vanish too.
  • Drag the roughness slider; compare how each mode blurs.
  • Enable Side mirrors in planar mode; compare the stronger reflections against the frame-time cost.
